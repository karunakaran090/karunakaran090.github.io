Consumer

From marketplaces and apparel to new forms of transportation and media, GV backs consumer companies that strive to make our daily lives better.

Enterprise

We’re drawn to founders who push the edge of what’s possible. Our investments span the technology stack, from applications to infrastructure, and across the enterprise spectrum from developer operations to security, data platforms, and beyond.

Life Science

Life sciences investments make up more than a third of GV’s portfolio. We invest across the entire healthcare spectrum, including care delivery, health IT, devices, diagnostics, and therapeutics. Our interests are broad, and we’re especially interested in companies at the intersection of health and information technology.

Frontier Tech

The future will be shaped by advances in AI, robotics and hardware, quantum computing, food and agriculture, and deep tech. We support founders with the expertise and drive to tackle the major global issues that affect us all.
