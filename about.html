My Story

Leblanc grew up in ​South Houston, Texas​. He learned various trades from his dad and neighbors as a young teen that include electrical, HVAC, welding, roofing, Autocad drafting, and woodworking. Early on, he understood the value of a dollar and eager to earn it himself.

Today, Leblanc lives in ​Keller, Texas​, is an engineer, full stack developer, change agent and technical lead capable of building teams and bringing multi-tenant SaaS products to market.

Leblanc has established his implementation abilities with various languages and platforms, generally using a test-driven development approach, and always using a continuous delivery process to improve quality and reduce software delivery time.

Leblanc's strong technical abilities, broad consulting experience (Including 5 years at Google), and creative business solutions give him an edge not often found.

Leblanc's usual activities include reading,​ growth ​hacking​, helping his​ ​customers​, sharing his thoughts on technology and startups, and instilling an always-be-learning principle in his kids.

Leblanc has a Bachelor of Science degree in​ ​Electronic Engineering Technology​ from Texas A&M University.
